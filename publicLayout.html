What's In Your 2022 Outlaw Class C Toy Hauler?

2022 Outlaw Class C Toy Hauler By Thor Motor Coach

Description
Traveling in a toy hauler is a unique experience. You get to take all the fun items with you that others have to leave at home. So you need some power, which is why Outlaw’s platform is a Ford® E-Series chassis. There’s a garage and an 8,000-pound hitch for whatever you want to bring along for the journey. So feel free to take that Motorcycle Trip; you’ve got an Outlaw.
